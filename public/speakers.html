Past Guest Speakers



Sean Chin

Product Designer, Uber

Sean graduated from the Information Systems program at Carnegie Mellon in 2012. He was always curious about how people think and are motivated by things, so he also took courses in Design, HCI, Decision Science and Business. After CMU, he worked as a Design Consultant at MAYA Design (A Boston Consulting Group company), helping Fortune 500 companies embrace the Human-Centered Design process. Sean then joined Duolingo as an early employee, leading the design for Duolingo.com and Duolingo for Schools. Today, Duolingo is the largest language learning platform in the world with over 150 million registered users. Now Sean works at Uber's Advanced Technologies group, responsible for the user experience of the Self-Driving Uber.
